## Why This Document Exists

The Gauge Kit (named in the session retro 2026-02-16) is the shared set of models, vocabulary, and references that
functions as our process gauge. Each term condenses a giant's insight into a word or phrase that carries an entire
architectural argument.

The retro said: "Each model is external — physics, mathematics, comics, information theory. Not invented by us.
Discovered, mapped, applied."

But that's only half the story. The Kit has three tiers:

1. **Giants** — external, stable, centuries/decades of proof. We stand on them.
2. **Constructions** — built by the team (Mårelius, Mårelius+Mellber), anchored to giants, tested across 38+ sessions.
   We built these.
3. **Narratives** — stories (Moore, Nolan, Wachowskis) that encode structural truth. One name carries an entire
   argument.
